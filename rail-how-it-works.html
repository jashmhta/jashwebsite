How your  international  payment works on  Rail’s  hybrid system

Partner

When you partner with Rail, you get access to our global network of banking partners, unlocking a suite of capabilities and currency support.

Initiate

You initiate the payment via the API (or your Rail dashboard) and we chart the most cost and time efficient path through our partner network, including identifying backup partners for redundancy.

Transfer

As needed, the payment will pass from a fiat currency into a stablecoin through an exchange to expedite the payment exponentially while costing you almost nothing in fees. We manage the compliance and tracking on transitions between fiat and stablecoins.

Success

The stablecoin settles into the worldwide fiat currency of your choice. Payments that took days and weeks now take hours or less, no wallets required.

Move money

Get started with infrastructure that’s fast, smarter and built for global reach. Let’s future-proof your payments together.
