About Rail

We're on a mission to make instant global money movement available to every business, everywhere.

Rail was founded to solve a fundamental problem: global payments are slow, expensive, and unreliable, and legacy infrastructure isn't keeping up with the pace of business.

Instead of layering over broken systems, our co-founders Bhanu Kohli (CEO, right) and Tarun Mistry (CTO, left) built Rail to deliver faster, more reliable cross-border payments by bridging traditional banking with the efficiencies of stablecoins and blockchain.

With a compliance-first approach, multi-bank redundancy, and seamless integration, Rail ensures businesses can move money globally with speed, affordability, and confidence.
